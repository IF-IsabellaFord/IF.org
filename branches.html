🌱 Branches of IF

Each branch of IF.org explores a critical domain of access, ethics, and education. These branches are student-led, mission-driven, and rooted in the power of shared knowledge.

🩺 IFMedical

A survival guide for premeds. IFMedical offers free MCAT resources, volunteering guides, and application strategy with a focus on underserved students. Visit ifmedical.github.io

⚖️ IFLaw

Focused on pre-law support, IFLaw will feature LSAT prep tools, law school timelines, and equity-centered legal literacy content. Launching soon.

📚 IFEdu

A branch dedicated to expanding access to education — for future teachers, curriculum designers, and student advocates. Resources coming in Fall 2025.

🗣 IFVoices

Media, storytelling, and lived experience. IFVoices publishes op-eds, interviews, and first-person narratives that challenge assumptions and elevate silenced voices.

💡 Future Branches
