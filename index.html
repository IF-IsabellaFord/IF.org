Welcome to IF.org

IF.org is a nonprofit built on the idea that everything starts with a question. We are dedicated to creating freely accessible, ethically driven resources across medicine, law, education, and more. Our mission is to empower thinkers, doers, and dreamers through the power of curiosity.

IF stands for always thinking, questioning, and exploring. If anything on this site contradicts that purpose, please contact us at contact@if.org.
