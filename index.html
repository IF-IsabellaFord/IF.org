Welcome to IF.org

IF.org is a student-led nonprofit committed to expanding access to education, ethics, and equity in high-impact spaces like medicine, law, and policy. We believe every movement starts with a question — that’s why we always begin with if.

What We Do

  • Build Toolkits: Free, open-access guides for underrepresented students in every field.
  • Support Branches: IFMedical, IFLaw, IFVoices, and more — all led by volunteers.
  • Host Conversations: Community events and digital media that challenge status quos.

Get Involved

IF is open to anyone who believes in asking better questions. Whether you're a student, mentor, educator, or curious thinker, there's a place for you here.
